About Us

PC & More computer shop

PC & More is a well-established computer shop located in Beirut, Lebanon. Founded in 2010, our shop has been providing high-quality pre-built desktops and laptops to customers for over a decade. Throughout the years, we have built a strong reputation for our expertise in the latest computer technology and our commitment to excellent customer service. At PC & More, we understand that each customer has unique needs, which is why we offer a wide range of products from top brands such as Dell, HP, Lenovo, and Asus. Our experienced professionals are always on hand to provide expert advice and guidance to help you make the right choice for your computing needs. In addition to our sales services, we also offer comprehensive repair and maintenance services for all types of computers. Our team of certified technicians is equipped with the latest tools and knowledge to diagnose and fix any issues with your device. At PC & More, we are proud to have served our loyal customers for over a decade and look forward to continuing to provide high-quality products and services for many more years to come. Visit us today and experience the convenience and expertise of our trusted computer shop.
